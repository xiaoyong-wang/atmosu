To the stars through difficulties

Space is the only way the human race can survive. Earth will not last indefinitely, but space will. We need to colonize the universe as fast as physically and humanly possible. The universe is infinite - overpopulation is not possible, and humanity would have no single point of failure. If you don't believe in space, you don't believe in humanity.

We have a mission. One that matters.

Our short-term mission is to ensure that humanity will begin to venture out into space and leave the small rock which it's currently confined to. By doing so, humanity can eventually prosper, get clues to the origins of the universe, discover intelligent alien life, and much more. Our long-term goal is to ensure that humanity expands to as many places as possible in the observable universe. By doing so, humanity's continued existence would be ensured. So, while some companies have a goal of lofting a bunch of satellites into orbit, we've decided to focus on something that is way more important.

WE WANT YOU TO HELP ADVANCE OUR SPECIES

We want you to help push our species, humanity, forward, by getting involved in anything STEM, because that's the future. Right now humanity is messing around. We operate very inefficiently and waste lots of time doing things that don't really matter. STEM is the future of humanity. Of the 1.8 million bachelor's degrees awarded in 2015–16, only about 331,000 (18%) were in STEM fields. That number is not nearly as high enough as it should be. We encourage you to pursue a career in a STEM field, so that we can have an unimaginable future.
