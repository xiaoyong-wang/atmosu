WARP DRIVE TECHNOLOGY

EXPAND LIFE BEYOND THE laniakea SUPERCLUSTER VIA WARP DRIVE TECHNOLOGY.

A warp drive or a drive enabling space warp is a hypothetical superluminal spacecraft propulsion system, which is seen in many science fiction works, most notably Star Trek and The Orville, and is a subject of ongoing physics research. The most well-known real warp drive concept is Alcubierre's warp drive. Alcubierre’s warp drive would work by creating a bubble of flat spacetime around the spaceship and curving spacetime around that bubble to reduce distances. The warp drive would require either negative mass or a ring of negative energy density to work. The only negative mass or negative energy that may exist in our universe is dark energy and/or dark matter. Unfortunately, we don't have the technology to manipulate dark matter/energy right now, or truly know what they are comprised of, as we're not yet advanced enough. Dark Energy could possess negative energy density, but we don't know. We'll have to work hard together as a species to find out.

According to our math, you'd have to be traveling 2.6280406e+13 m/s, or, 87,662 times the speed of light, to travel 1 light year in 6 minutes. We believe that we'd need to have the capability to travel at such speeds if we, humanity, would like to have a significantly-increased chance at discovering the origins of the universe, finding and observing intelligent alien life, discovering complex phenomena in space, and surviving in the long-term. It may seem impossible right now, and it is extremely difficult, but eventually, humanity could find a way to make warp drives a reality, despite the unimaginable amounts of energy they require, and the continued existence of humanity would be ensured. But without your (humanity's) help, it cannot be done. Warp Drive technology right now, as mentioned, seems virtually impossible to achieve, and it might even be physically impossible, however, "If something is important enough you should try, even if the probable outcome is failure." - Elon Musk.

Faster than light travel in space

As far as we know, one of the only ways we can travel faster than the speed of light is via a warp drive, like the Alcubierre Warp Drive. Faster than light travel is essential for humanity in the long-term. The entire universe can never be fully explored, as it's infinite, but warp drives allow us to explore a lot more of it. Warp Drives could uncover endless mysteries in our universe. They could also potentially allow us to have instant communication across great distances.

Enabling deep exploration

With Warp Drives, we can travel across the universe at a much faster rate. We must explore the cosmos if we want any chance at finding the origins of everything, discovering intelligent alien life, and more. If you had the choice to either live in a universe where we are still using traditional combustion-powered rockets, or, a universe where we are using warp-drive-powered spaceships exploring places we could have never imagined, which would you choose?
